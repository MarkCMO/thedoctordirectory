About Psychiatry

Psychiatrists are medical doctors who specialize in mental health, diagnosing and treating conditions including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and PTSD. They can prescribe medications and provide psychotherapy, offering comprehensive mental health treatment.
