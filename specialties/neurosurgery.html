About Neurosurgery

Neurosurgeons treat conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Procedures range from brain tumor removal and aneurysm clipping to spinal decompression, deep brain stimulation, and epilepsy surgery. Elite neurosurgeons specialize in minimally invasive approaches, awake craniotomy techniques, and stereotactic radiosurgery that minimize recovery time and maximize outcomes.

EliteMD features 498 peer-verified neurosurgery specialists across 21 states. Our neurosurgery doctors have an average of 15 years of experience, 76 publications, and a 4.60 average patient rating.
