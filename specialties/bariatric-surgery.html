About Bariatric Surgery

Bariatric surgeons perform weight-loss procedures including gastric bypass, sleeve gastrectomy, and duodenal switch. Modern bariatric surgery emphasizes minimally invasive and robotic techniques with proven long-term outcomes for weight loss and resolution of obesity-related conditions like type 2 diabetes, sleep apnea, and metabolic syndrome.

EliteMD features 498 peer-verified bariatric surgery specialists across 33 states. Our bariatric surgery doctors have an average of 15 years of experience, 81 publications, and a 4.61 average patient rating.
