About Internal Medicine

Internal medicine physicians (internists) specialize in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases. They manage complex, chronic conditions and coordinate care across multiple organ systems. Top internists combine broad medical knowledge with diagnostic expertise to provide comprehensive adult healthcare.
