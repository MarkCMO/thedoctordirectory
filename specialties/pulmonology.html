About Pulmonology

Pulmonologists specialize in diseases of the respiratory system including the lungs, airways, and chest. They treat conditions such as COPD, asthma, lung cancer, pulmonary fibrosis, and sleep-disordered breathing using medications, procedures, and ventilator management.
