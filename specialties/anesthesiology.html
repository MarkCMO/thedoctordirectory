About Anesthesiology

Anesthesiologists are physicians who specialize in perioperative care, pain management, and critical care medicine. They ensure patient safety during surgery through careful anesthesia administration and monitoring, and many also specialize in chronic pain management.

EliteMD features 91 peer-verified anesthesiology specialists across 1 states. Our anesthesiology doctors have an average of 19 years of experience, 77 publications, and a 4.64 average patient rating.
