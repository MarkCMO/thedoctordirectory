About Urology

Urologists treat conditions of the urinary tract and male reproductive system. This includes prostate cancer surgery, kidney cancer treatment, kidney stone management, and incontinence procedures. Elite urologists utilize robotic-assisted surgery, focal therapy, and advanced imaging to provide the least invasive and most effective treatments.

EliteMD features 499 peer-verified urology specialists across 23 states. Our urology doctors have an average of 16 years of experience, 77 publications, and a 4.61 average patient rating.
