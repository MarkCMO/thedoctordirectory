About Family Medicine

Family medicine doctors provide comprehensive healthcare for patients of all ages, from newborns to seniors. They manage acute illnesses, chronic diseases, and preventive care. The best family physicians build long-term relationships with patients and coordinate referrals to specialists when needed.

EliteMD features 300 peer-verified family medicine specialists across 1 states. Our family medicine doctors have an average of 17 years of experience, 84 publications, and a 4.59 average patient rating.
