About Ophthalmology

Ophthalmologists are eye doctors who perform surgery and treat diseases of the eye. Procedures include cataract surgery, LASIK and refractive surgery, glaucoma treatment, retinal surgery, and corneal transplantation. Leading ophthalmologists utilize femtosecond laser technology, micro-invasive glaucoma surgery (MIGS), and gene therapy for retinal diseases.

EliteMD features 499 peer-verified ophthalmology specialists across 20 states. Our ophthalmology doctors have an average of 16 years of experience, 75 publications, and a 4.60 average patient rating.
