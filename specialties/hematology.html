About Hematology

Hematologists specialize in blood disorders including anemia, blood clotting disorders, leukemia, lymphoma, and sickle cell disease. Many hematologists also subspecialize in oncology, treating blood cancers with chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and bone marrow transplantation.
