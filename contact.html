Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?
Our patient concierge team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent inquiries about finding a specialist, we aim to respond within 4 hours during business hours (9am-6pm EST).
Can you help me find a specific type of doctor?
Absolutely. Our concierge team specializes in matching patients with the right specialist for their specific condition. Contact us with your condition, location preference, and any other requirements, and we'll provide personalized recommendations.
Is my information kept confidential?
Yes. All communications through EliteMD are HIPAA-compliant. Your personal health information is encrypted and shared only with the specific doctor you choose to contact. We never sell or share patient data.
How do I get listed as a doctor on EliteMD?
Physicians can apply for listing by visiting our For Doctors page or contacting us directly. We require NPI verification, active board certification, and peer nominations as part of our vetting process.
