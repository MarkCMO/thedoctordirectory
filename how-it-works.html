Finding the Best Doctor Has Never Been Easier

EliteMD simplifies the process of finding and connecting with top-rated medical specialists. Our platform features 7,402+ peer-verified doctors across 15 specialties, all accessible through an intuitive search and inquiry system. Here's how it works:

Step 1

Search by Condition, Specialty, or Location

Start by searching our directory of 7,402+ verified specialists. You can filter by medical specialty (such as orthopedic surgery, neurosurgery, or oncology), by specific condition (like ACL tear, brain tumor, or breast cancer), or by state.

Every doctor profile includes detailed information about their education, board certifications, years of experience, number of procedures performed, research publications, patents, innovations, hospital affiliations, and patient ratings.

Step 2

Submit a Confidential Inquiry

Found the right specialist? Submit a confidential inquiry through their profile. Your message is securely delivered through our HIPAA-compliant platform — we never share your email, phone, or medical details with anyone except the doctor you choose to contact.

You can send inquiries for standard consultations, second opinions, or through our exclusive doctor-to-patient travel program where the specialist comes to you.

Step 3

Get Connected and Schedule Your Consultation

Doctors on EliteMD typically respond within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a personalized response addressing your condition and next steps. From there, you can schedule an in-office consultation, virtual visit, or travel consultation.

Our patient concierge team is available to help coordinate logistics, especially for our travel program consultations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to use EliteMD as a patient?
EliteMD is completely free for patients. There are no fees to search our directory, view doctor profiles, or submit inquiries to specialists.
How are doctor ratings calculated?
Our ratings combine peer evaluations from other physicians (peer rating), patient satisfaction scores, clinical outcome data, and reviews. This multi-source approach provides a more comprehensive and reliable rating than patient reviews alone.
What is the doctor-to-patient travel program?

How do I submit an inquiry to a doctor?
Visit any doctor's profile page on EliteMD and click 'Send Inquiry' or 'Request Consultation.' Fill in your condition details and contact information. Your inquiry is securely delivered to the doctor's dashboard, and most doctors respond within 24-48 hours.
Is my medical information secure?
Yes. All data on EliteMD is encrypted in transit and at rest. Our platform is HIPAA-compliant, and your personal health information is only shared with the specific doctor you contact.
Can I get a second opinion through EliteMD?
Absolutely. Many patients use EliteMD specifically to find top specialists for second opinions on diagnoses or treatment plans. You can submit inquiries to multiple doctors to compare perspectives.
