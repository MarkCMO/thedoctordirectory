Our Mission: Find the Best Doctor for Every Patient

EliteMD was founded with a simple but ambitious goal: to help every patient find the best possible doctor for their specific condition. In a healthcare system with over one million licensed physicians in the United States, finding the right specialist can be overwhelming. EliteMD solves this by curating a directory of only the top 1% of doctors and surgeons — peer-verified, innovation-tracked, and concierge-connected.

Unlike general doctor directories that list every physician with a valid license, EliteMD is selective. We evaluate doctors on clinical outcomes, peer nominations, research contributions, innovation, patient satisfaction, and board certifications. Our directory features 7,402+ elite specialists across 15 medical specialties in 47 states.

How We Verify Doctors

Every doctor on EliteMD goes through a rigorous verification process before being listed in our directory. This multi-step vetting ensures that patients can trust every specialist on our platform.

01

Peer Nomination

Doctors are nominated by fellow physicians who have directly observed their clinical skills, surgical outcomes, and patient care. Peer verification is the gold standard in identifying top medical talent because doctors know which colleagues they would trust with their own family's care.

02

Credential Verification

We verify every doctor's credentials through the NPPES NPI Registry, state medical boards, and specialty board certification databases. This includes confirming active medical licenses, board certifications, fellowship training, and hospital privileges.

03

Outcomes Analysis

We analyze clinical outcomes data including complication rates, patient satisfaction scores, and procedure volumes. Doctors with consistently excellent outcomes and high patient satisfaction are prioritized in our directory rankings.

04

Innovation Tracking

We track each doctor's contributions to medical innovation including published research, patents, novel surgical techniques, and participation in clinical trials. Innovation-driven physicians often provide the most advanced treatment options.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EliteMD?
EliteMD is a curated medical directory featuring the top 1% of doctors and surgeons across the United States. Every physician on our platform is peer-verified, with tracked clinical outcomes, innovations, and patient satisfaction scores.
How are doctors selected for EliteMD?
Doctors are selected through a rigorous multi-step process that includes peer nominations from other physicians, verification of board certifications through the NPI Registry, analysis of clinical outcomes and patient satisfaction data, review of publications and innovations, and confirmation of active medical licenses.
Is EliteMD free for patients?
Yes, EliteMD is completely free for patients. You can search our directory, view doctor profiles, and submit inquiries at no cost. We are funded by premium physician listings.
How does the doctor-to-patient travel program work?
Our travel program connects patients with specialists who are willing to travel to the patient's location for consultations or procedures. Patients request a travel consultation through the doctor's profile, and our concierge team coordinates logistics including scheduling, travel arrangements, and facility coordination.
How do I contact a doctor through EliteMD?
You can contact any doctor on EliteMD by visiting their profile page and clicking 'Send Inquiry' or 'Request Consultation.' Your inquiry is securely routed through our HIPAA-compliant platform to the doctor's dashboard.
